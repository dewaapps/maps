Assassin's Creep.

Assassin's Creep is a open-world parkour map for minecraft. The theme of the map is heavily influenced by the video game series Assassin's Creed.

The main objective of Assassin's Creep is to find every Wool Block (9 in total). To obtain these blocks, you must complete each parkour challenge. Once you have all 9, you are able to enter the final dungeon.

There are some extra things to find around the map as well, including hidden feathers. and special wanted posters.

Make sure the map is on peaceful mode, to avoid mobs interfering, and the usual rules apply (No breaking/placing blocks or cheating).
